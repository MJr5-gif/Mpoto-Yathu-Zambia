Reviews

"Mpoto Yathu, Lusaka served some of the most amazing food I had in Zambia. Every dish was full of flavor, authentic, and beautifully prepared. The welcoming atmosphere made the experience even more special. This is a meal and a place I will always remember from my time in Zambia. Highly recommended for anyone looking to enjoy delicious local cuisine in Lusaka. Food: 5/5 | Service: 5/5 | Atmosphere: 5/5" — Sunita Nazareth

"Mpoto Yatu does the local Zambian cuisines just the way you love them, without the risk of food poisoning like from a random market. They also serve healthy portions, I have seldom managed to finish my plate. If you have visitors from outside Zambia and you want them to try local food, look no further." — Gibson Chitunda

"One of the best local restaurants I’ve visited in Lusaka! The grill is absolutely amazing — perfectly done and served sizzling hot throughout my lunch. Service was impressively fast (probably the fastest I've ever had), and the waiter was friendly and attentive. To top it off, there was live music — a guitarist with a beautiful voice, which made the meal even more enjoyable. It happened to be Independence Day, and the atmosphere was simply perfect. The only downside is that they close quite early (around 3pm), which is a pity for such a great spot. Still, highly recommended for anyone who loves good food, good vibes, and great service!" — Simo K
